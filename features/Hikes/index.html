What is hiking?

Hiking is the most fun way for us to explore a destination. You come to places where many travelers - due to the long distance - do not come. Many travelers hike on their vacation or trip because you get to see places that are sometimes only accessible by a long and sometimes difficult hike (think of the Trolltunga in Norway). In addition, hiking gives you a kind of relaxation because you are in nature. But hiking, walking, trekking and climbing are all used interchangeably. In this article we explain what hiking exactly means and you will find other useful hiking tips to be well prepared!

Be prepared!

  • Navigation tools such as a map and compass
  • Headlamp
  • Sun protection
  • First-aid kit
  • Knife or multi-tool
  • Fire
  • Shelter
  • Extra food
  • Extra water
  • Extra clothes
  • Hiking backpack
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (think moisture-wicking and layers)
  • Hiking boots or shoes
